Intro

WD42 is a Hobart monthly evening meetup of web developers, designers and tinkerers.

About

Each month we have around 4 local speakers talking about a piece of technology, their latest mashup/startup or show us some of their secret technical sauce.

Attendance is free and drinks/nibbles are provided!

Where/When

We meet on the last Wednesday of every month from 6pm at The Typewriter Factory.

The Typewriter Factory,
Level 3, 13-17 Castray Esplanade,
Hobart, Tasmania,
